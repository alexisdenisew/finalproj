Meet Alex

Alex Celedon is a junior at Archbishop Molloy High School. For all of her life, Alex has lived in Queens, one of the most diverse places in America. Alex can no longer standby and watch people act discriminatory towards women of color. She hopes to use technology to end stereotypes and have a positive impact on other women like herself.

Meet Alexis

Alexis Williams was born on May 14 and was raised in northern New Jersey. She loves school and challenging herself with AP and honors classes. Alexis always finds things to do for fun whether it be writing, reading, singing, dancing, or more. Her favorite activities include singing and dancing to music, writing prose and sonnets, reading books. Coming from a town that is predominantly Caucasian, she wanted to be a part of something that would create a safe haven for black women filled with love, unity, and positivity, which is how Bubble was born.

Meet Miriam

Miriam Shapir is a 16 year old rising junior in high school. She was born and raised in Brooklyn, New York in a modern orthodox Jewish home. From subways to summer camps, she has been looked at or treated differently because of her religion. Her hobbies include weight-lifting and reading. She is also very interested in entering the technology industry in the future.

Purpose

Bubble exists as an online community of women of color dedicated to celebrating each other's successes. This blog is to promote positivity and unity among women of color. This is done by posting articles with positive news, achievements, and promoting small businesses.
